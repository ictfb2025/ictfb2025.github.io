International conference on tiling and Fourier bases (2025 Sep.15-- Sep.19)

An International Workshop on tiling and Fourier bases will be held at Sep. 15-19, 2025 in Xi'an. The focus of this workshop will be on the structure of tiling and spectral sets (the Fuglede problem). Related areas are also welcome.

The expected arrival and departure dates will be 15th Sep. 2025 and 19th Sep. 2025, respectively.

Participation in this conference is by invitation only.
